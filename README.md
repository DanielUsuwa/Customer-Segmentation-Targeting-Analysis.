Business Problem
How can a company identify high-value customer segments and prioritize them to improve targeting and support business growth?

Approach

Segmented customers based on behavioural and lifestyle data
Identified key drivers such as health orientation, planning behaviour, and visit frequency
Evaluated segment size, penetration, and strategic importance

Key Insights

Health-conscious customers drive the highest engagement and penetration
Distinct customer segments show clear differences in behaviour and value
Behavioural patterns are stronger indicators than demographics
Certain segments offer higher long-term potential for retention and growth

Business Impact

Identified priority segments for targeting and expansion
Supported data-driven marketing strategy decisions
Highlighted lower-value segments to deprioritize
